Oakwood Leisure Centre

Scenario

TechTeam the onsite technical team have called you in as an expert in cyber security. They have had a large spike of incidents and need some senior assistance in resolving the issues. They are normally sufficient, but die to the high number of incidents of recent, they are struggling. Logan, one of the managers has suggested that they may have been hacked, following the companies recent unpopular decision to returf the football pitch with astro turf, and close the gym after 8pm, due to the high number of incidents of innapropriate behaviour. The company has also recently had a large number of new staff join the team, and they have been using the network to access the internet and their emails.

Symptoms and Issues:

  • Slow Performance & Freezing Terminals – The reception and managerial computers frequently crash or freeze.
  • Intermittent Wi-Fi Drops – Customers and staff report unstable Wi-Fi connections.
  • Vending Machine Errors – Machines fail to process payments or dispense items incorrectly.
  • HVAC Malfunctions – Heating and ventilation operate sporadically, with unexpected temperature fluctuations.
  • Swimming Pool Control Issues – The automated chlorine levels and filtration settings reset unpredictably, leading to safety concerns.
  • Security System Disruptions – CCTV cameras are intermittently offline, and automated door locks fail to engage properly.
  • Unauthorized Network Traffic – Unusual network activity is detected, including unknown IP addresses attempting to access restricted systems.
